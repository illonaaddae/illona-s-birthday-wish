About Illona

🎖️ Illona Addae is an aspiring software engineer, Founder and Executive Director at Slint Tech, diversity advocate, and mentor committed to empowering women and underrepresented communities through technology. Based in Ghana, she is passionate about mastering front-end and back-end development with React, Node.js, MongoDB, and Python, alongside exploring AI, Machine Learning, and Robotics.🎖️

Mission & Dreams

Illona’s mission is to challenge the status quo, bridging gender gaps in technology and creating ethical AI solutions that benefit society. With a background shaped by perseverance, she dreams of contributing to Robotics and AI innovations for real-world impact. Because she believe in a world where opportunities are equal, dreams are boundless, and success is driven by determination, hard work, and talent, not defined by gender. And that technology should be accessible to everyone, regardless of their background.
